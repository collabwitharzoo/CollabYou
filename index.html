Questions

Still have questions?

Our creator relations team is available Monday–Friday, 10am–7pm IST. We typically respond within 4 hours.

What follower count do I need to apply?
We accept creators from 5,000 followers upward across all platforms. We evaluate engagement rate, content quality, and audience authenticity more heavily than raw follower count.
Is there a fee to join?
Absolutely not. Applying and joining is completely free. We earn a commission only when you earn — typically 15–20% of the campaign fee, disclosed transparently in every contract.
How quickly will I get my first brand offer?
Most approved creators receive their first brand match within 2–4 weeks. Speed depends on your niche and current campaign availability. Some creators receive offers within days of approval.
Can I work with multiple agencies?
Yes. We do not require exclusivity. You are free to pursue independent deals or work with other agencies, as long as you honour any brand exclusivity windows in individual campaign contracts.
What platforms do you support?
Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X, Pinterest, LinkedIn, Snapchat, Twitch, and emerging platforms. Multi-platform creators are especially attractive to our brand partners.
How and when are payments processed?
Payments are processed within 14 business days of content approval, via bank transfer, PayPal, or Wise in your local currency. All tax documentation is handled through our creator portal.
